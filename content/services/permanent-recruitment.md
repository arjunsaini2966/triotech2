## Our Recruitment Process

### Phase 1: Discovery (Week 1)
**Understand Your Needs**
- Job description collaboration
- Role requirements definition
- Team culture assessment
- Compensation benchmarking

### Phase 2: Sourcing (Week 1-2)
**Find Top Candidates**
- Active candidate outreach
- Tap into passive talent pool
- Utilize our network of 10,000+ IT professionals
- Review referrals and applications

### Phase 3: Screening (Week 2)
**Initial Vetting**
- Resume screening
- Phone screenings
- Initial technical assessment
- Culture fit evaluation

### Phase 4: Presentation (Week 2-3)
**Shortlist Delivery**
- Present 3-5 top candidates
- Detailed candidate profiles
- Technical evaluation reports
- Salary expectations and availability

### Phase 5: Interviews (Week 3)
**Client Interviews**
- Coordinate interview schedules
- Provide interview guides
- Collect feedback
- Facilitate second rounds

### Phase 6: Offer & Closing (Week 3-4)
**Seal the Deal**
- Salary negotiation support
- Offer letter preparation
- Background verification
- Notice period management

### Phase 7: Onboarding (Week 4+)
**Smooth Transition**
- Onboarding coordination
- First-day support
- 30-60-90 day check-ins
- Address any concerns
