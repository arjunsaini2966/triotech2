## Our Hiring Process

### Step 1: Apply

Submit your resume and cover letter through our careers portal.

### Step 2: Initial Screening

Our HR team reviews your application and conducts an initial phone screening.

### Step 3: Interview

Meet with the hiring manager and team members (1-2 rounds).

### Step 4: Assessment (if applicable)

Some roles may require skills assessment or case study.

### Step 5: Offer

Successful candidates receive an offer letter with details.

### Step 6: Onboarding

Welcome to the team! Comprehensive onboarding and training provided.

**Timeline**: Typically 2-3 weeks from application to offer.
